FACS Datasets for Training & Testing

The Facial Action Coding System (FACS) is the gold standard for measuring facial expressions. It describes facial movements as combinations of Action Units (AUs) - individual muscle movements. Professional emotion detection models are trained on FACS-labeled datasets.

FACS Emotion Reference (with Action Units)

Each emotion corresponds to specific Action Unit combinations

:) Happy / Joy

Duchenne smile with genuine eye involvement. Crow's feet appear around eyes.

FACS Action Units

AU6 - Cheek Raise AU12 - Lip Corner Pull
  • Raised cheeks
  • Lip corners pulled up and back
  • Eyes narrow with crow's feet
:( Sad

Inner brow raise, drooping mouth corners, often with lowered gaze.

FACS Action Units

AU1 - Inner Brow Raise AU4 - Brow Lowerer AU15 - Lip Corner Depress
  • Inner eyebrows raised
  • Lip corners pulled down
  • Lower lip pushed up
>:( Angry

Lowered brows, tightened lips, narrowed eyes with hard stare.

FACS Action Units

AU4 - Brow Lowerer AU5 - Upper Lid Raise AU7 - Lid Tightener AU23 - Lip Tightener
  • Eyebrows lowered and drawn together
  • Upper eyelids raised
  • Lips pressed together
:O Fear

Wide eyes, raised and drawn together brows, horizontal stretched mouth.

FACS Action Units

AU1 - Inner Brow Raise AU2 - Outer Brow Raise AU4 - Brow Lowerer AU5 - Upper Lid Raise AU20 - Lip Stretch
  • Eyebrows raised and pulled together
  • Upper eyelids raised (white visible)
  • Mouth stretched horizontally
:0 Surprise

Raised eyebrows (curved), wide open eyes, dropped jaw.

FACS Action Units

AU1 - Inner Brow Raise AU2 - Outer Brow Raise AU5 - Upper Lid Raise AU26 - Jaw Drop
  • Entire eyebrow raised high
  • Eyes wide open
  • Jaw dropped open
:P Disgust

Nose wrinkle, raised upper lip, lowered brows, narrowed eyes.

FACS Action Units

AU9 - Nose Wrinkler AU10 - Upper Lip Raise AU4 - Brow Lowerer
  • Nose wrinkled
  • Upper lip raised
  • Chin raised
:| Neutral

Relaxed face with no strong activation of facial muscles.

FACS Action Units

No AUs Active
  • Relaxed forehead
  • Eyes at rest
  • Mouth closed, relaxed

Testing Checklist

  • Application starts without errors
  • Faces detected correctly (not icons/emojis)
  • Bounding boxes appear around faces
  • Multi-model predictions display (DeepFace, OpenCV)
  • Confidence scores are reasonable (>50%)
  • Facial mesh overlay toggles properly
  • Multiple faces detected simultaneously
  • MediaPipe blendshapes working
  • Emotion smoothing prevents flickering
  • Session logging works correctly
